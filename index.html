about us

Tech. GATEWAY
MOVING FORWARD WITH TECHNOLOGY
Don’t think big, Think wise

To bring in technical ideas from any source on a single platform to develop an idea into a product. To help students develop their ideas into products. The technology gateway society (TechGATEWAY) is established with a vision to provide a single platform to different stakeholders to develop an environment of work, which should be in the same orientation as the current market demands from our students. The TechGATEWAY intends to provide different opportunities to our students to help them craft their idea into a product. The society will address different industrial segments/departments to bring in their problems to formulate a proper solution with the help of our students. Different pieces of training will be conducted to aware students about the current market requirements and skills sets. TechGATEWAY society will help students to market their project (product) with the help of SSBC at COMSATS University Islamabad-Abbottabad Campus. We on behalf of TechGATEWAY welcomes everyone/anyone to bring in their ideas/problemsso that we can discuss it and build a team to get its solution.
