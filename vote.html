Official GSA Election Info

Why I am running

As a 3rd-year PhD candidate and previous staff member at the MacOdrum Library, I understand the academic landscape at Carleton from both a researcher's and a student's perspective. I am running as an Independent to ensure that graduate academic advocacy remains focused entirely on student needs and institutional excellence.

My Commitment to You

1. Strong Academic Advocacy

I will act as your dedicated voice on the University Senate and Graduate Council, ensuring that policy changes prioritize graduate student well-being and research integrity.

2. Empowering the Academic Caucus

I will ensure every department has active representation in the Graduate Academic Caucus, bridging the gap between departmental needs and the GSA Executive.

3. Accessible Student Aid

I am committed to providing clear, resourceful assistance for students navigating academic or administrative challenges within their departments.

Experience You Can Trust

  • Leadership: Former VP of the Graduate Student Council (MBZUAI) and Co-Founder/President of The Carleton High Tea Society CHTS.
  • Teaching: Experienced Teaching Assistant for multiple graduate and undergraduate courses, including COMP 5801H, DATA 5000W, and COMP 4010A, among others.
  • Research: Author of multiple peer-reviewed publications in Generative AI and Accessibility, with work featured in major venues like IEEE SMC, MICCAI, and ICAI
